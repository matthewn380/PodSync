# 🎵 PodSync - Manage your classic iPod music library

[![](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download_PodSync-Blue?style=for-the-badge&logo=github)](https://github.com/matthewn380/PodSync)

PodSync lets you move your music collection onto classic iPod hardware. It turns your modern music library into a portable format. You can drag music files from your computer and drop them onto your iPod. The software handles the conversion and file transfer steps for you. 

## 🛠️ System Requirements

You need a computer running Windows 10 or Windows 11. Your computer requires at least 4GB of RAM and 100MB of free space on your hard drive. Ensure your iPod uses a standard 30-pin dock connector or USB cable connection. Keep your iPod power cable nearby during the sync process.

## 💾 How to Install PodSync

Follow these steps to install the software on your Windows computer:

1. Visit the [official releases page](https://github.com/matthewn380/PodSync).
2. Look for the latest file titled PodSync-Setup.exe.
3. Click the file to start the download.
4. Open the downloaded file once the process finishes.
5. Follow the on-screen prompts to complete the installation.
6. Launch the application from your Start Menu.

## 🎧 Connecting Your iPod

Plug your iPod into your computer using your USB cable. Windows should recognize the device as a removable drive within a few seconds. If the device does not appear, check the cable connection. You may need to put the iPod into Disk Mode if the computer fails to detect the storage partition. PodSync will automatically scan for connected Apple hardware once you open the main window. 

## 📂 Syncing Your Music

The interface shows your iPod storage on the right side. Your computer folder view stays on the left. Find the music folder you want to move. Select your songs or albums and drag them into the PodSync window. The transfer bar displays the progress for each file. Wait for the green checkmark to confirm the success of each song. Do not disconnect the USB cable while the transfer bar shows active movement.

## ⚙️ Advanced Settings

You can change how PodSync creates your library. The settings menu allows you to choose the destination folder for audiobooks. You can toggle the scrobble feature if you use a Last.fm account. This feature tracks your listening habits and saves your data. Update your Last.fm credentials in the settings tab to enable this function. 

## 🛠️ Troubleshooting Common Issues

If the software stops responding, restart the application. Ensure you have the latest drivers for your USB ports. If the iPod shows a sync error, eject the device safely through the taskbar and reconnect the cable. Try a different USB port if the connection remains unstable. Reset the iPod by holding the Menu and Center buttons if the device freezes during a file write operation.

## 🧩 Features

- Drag-and-Drop: Move files from your desktop to the iPod with ease.
- Scrobbling: Send your listening history to your Last.fm profile.
- Media Support: Sync music, podcasts, and audiobooks in one place.
- Open Interface: View exactly which files occupy your iPod drive space.
- Free Access: Use the software without monthly fees or hidden costs.

## 💡 Best Practices

Keep your iPod battery charged above 50 percent during long sync sessions. Disconnect the device using the Eject button in the bottom corner of PodSync before you pull the cable out. This step protects your music files from corruption. Rename your files on your computer before you sync to ensure the iPod displays the correct titles. Organize your music folders by artist name for easier navigation on the iPod screen.
